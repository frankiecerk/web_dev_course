Grace Hopper

Grace Hopper was an earlier pioneer in the field of Computer Science. She is credited for inventing the first programming language, COBOL

Ada Lovelance

Ada Lovelace was was a mathematician and writer, known for her work on the "Analytical Engine" - an early theoretical computer. She is widely known as the world's first computer programmer, and is credited with writing the world's first computer program

Katherine Johnson

Creola Katherine Johnson was mathematician whose calculations of orbital mechanics as a NASA employee were critical to the success of the first U.S. spaceflights. She earned a reputation for mastering complex manual calculations and helped pioneer the use of computers to perform the tasks.
