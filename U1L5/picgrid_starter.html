Computer Science Greats

Grace hopper - Inventor of the first programming language

Katherine Johnson - NASA mathematician known as the "human computer"

Don Knuth - Author of "The Art of Computer Programming"

Ada Lovelace - The first computer programmer

Alan Turing - The father of theoretical computer science.

Gladys West - developed mathematical models of the shape of the Earth that were later incorporated into the Global Positioning System (GPS)

Claude Shannon - father of information theory

Frances Allen - pioneer in compiler optimization

Margaret Hamilton - lead software engineer for the Apollo 11 program
